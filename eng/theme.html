Theme Lessons

Designed for children above 49 months and older, our thematic lessons incorporate Havruta-style self-directed activities, encouraging a broader worldview through various direct and indirect experiences.

01
Class Harpatka

Harpatka (meaning “adventure” in Hebrew) is conducted in both English and Korean, designed to expand global thinking through the Havruta method.
The class supports long-term development in areas such as literacy, critical thinking, and cognitive flexibility—laying a strong foundation for future learning.

02
Class Bitahon

In collaboration with a UK-trained theatre director, Bitahon offers a self-led theatre program where children interpret a wide range of texts—from picture books to classic literature—through their own creative lens.
Children take the lead in every step of the theatrical process, from character selection and script-writing to prop creation and stage design, culminating in a performance that is entirely their own.
