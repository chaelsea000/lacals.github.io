LaCalS Mission

LaCalS has been founded with the heartfelt hope of giving wings to each child’s uniqueness—enabling them to soar higher, farther, and-most importantly-bravely and joyfully. We believe that every child carries within them a spark of originality, and it is our mission to nurture that spark into confident self-expression, creative exploration, and emotional resilience. At LaCalS, developmental science meets the arts to build a stage where children’s voices are heard, their imaginations come alive, and their potential takes flight.
